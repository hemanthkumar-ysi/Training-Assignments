Chaitanya Bharathi Institute of Technology (CBIT) is a private engineering college located in Gandipet, Hyderabad, Telangana, India. Established in 1979, the institution is affiliated with Osmania University and is recognized as one of the prominent engineering colleges in the region.

CBIT offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering, management, and computer applications. Popular programs include Computer Science Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering, Civil Engineering, and Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. The college focuses on providing strong technical education combined with practical learning and research opportunities.

The campus spans a large urban area and provides modern laboratories, libraries, and academic facilities. CBIT received autonomous status in 2013 and maintains accreditation from national academic bodies. The institution aims to address emerging technological needs through quality education and advanced research.

Student activities at CBIT include technical festivals, innovation clubs, Model United Nations conferences, and TEDx events organized by students. The college has produced notable alumni across technology, business, and entertainment industries and continues to contribute skilled engineering professionals to the industry.
